Mission Statement:

"Stand up for yourself! Show courage! Don’t be afraid! You are not the only one!"

Our mission statement expresses how bullying has become such a horrific part of anyone’s life.
This tragedy can be prevented if we show that we have courage and not be afraid to stand up
for ourselves. This statement shows that we need to push forward and raise awareness for this cause,
for the benefit of our younger generations. Bullying will never stop, but we can prevent it!
